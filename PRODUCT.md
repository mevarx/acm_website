# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Students, faculty, industry partners, and potential new members of the ACM Student Chapter at SRM University AP.

## Product Purpose
To serve as the digital home for the collegiate tech organization affiliated with the Association for Computing Machinery. It needs to attract new members, showcase the chapter's prestige, and host events, blogs, and community life.

## Brand Personality
Professional but approachable. A prestigious academic computing community. Think MIT CSAIL meets a well-run university club.

## Anti-references
- Startup aesthetics
- SaaS cliché elements (generic teal, large rounded square icons above every heading, hero-metric template)
- Purple-to-blue gradients
- Pure black/white (tint neutrals instead)
- Nested cards
- Bounce/elastic motion easing
- Inter as the only font
- Fake terminal UI (unless fully functional)

## Design Principles
- **Deep Hierarchy & Structure**: Clear typographic scales and spatial rhythms.
- **Academic Prestige over Startup Hype**: Serious, technical feel anchored in strong typography and precise layout rather than floating glassmorphic blobs.
- **Intentional Motion**: Purposeful, subtle motion without bouncy reflexes.
- **Authenticity over Placeholder**: Use real data or remove the section. Never use grey text on coloured backgrounds.
